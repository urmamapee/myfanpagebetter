SGT. FROG

This is a webpage all about the anime named Keroro Gunsou (Sgt. Frog)

HISTORY

Sgt. Frog began as a comedy manga series in April of 1999, written and illustrated by Mine Yoshizaki, and was given an anime series in April of 2004. The anime ended in April of 2011, but the manga is still ongoing. The anime is considered a classic in Japan, even to this day.

PREMISE

One day in 2004 Japan, a mysterious frog-like alien infiltrates the household of the Hinata family with a plan to take over all of Earth, or according to him, Pekopon. His cover gets blown by siblings Natsumi and Fuyuki, and as a result, he gets abandoned by his mothership. Sergeant Keroro, and his platoon who got abandoned as well, are forced to stay on Pekopon and live with the Pekoponians, or humans. Keroro and his platoon get into various hijinks trying to complete their mission to take over earth.
